Parbhani Ki Biryani

Chicken Biryani is a fragrant and flavorful rice dish made with aromatic basmati rice, tender chicken, and a blend of spices. It’s one of the most loved dishes in South Asian cuisine.

The most loved dishes in South Asian cuisine !!

Prep Time :30 Minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Whole spices (bay leaf, cloves, cinnamon, cardamom)
  • 2 tsp biryani masala powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil or ghee
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Wash and soak the rice for 30 minutes. Cook until 70% done, then set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pot, fry sliced onions until golden brown. Remove some for garnishing.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute.
  4. Add chicken pieces, turmeric, chili powder, and salt. Cook until chicken is half done.
  5. Mix in tomatoes, yogurt, and biryani masala. Cook until chicken is fully cooked.
  6. Layer rice over the chicken mixture. Sprinkle fried onions, mint, and coriander.
  7. Cover with a tight lid and cook on low heat (“dum”) for 15–20 minutes.
  8. Fluff gently and serve hot with raita or salad.
